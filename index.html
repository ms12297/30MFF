Welcome to our 30 Minute Film Festival Website!

We were tasked with creating a short film on any subject. Abdul and I (Zaeem) were paired up for this project. While filming, we discovered something unbelievable and that pleasant surprise is now the topic of our short-film.

Synopsis

As two students begin planning a "Day in the Life of an NYUAD Student" film, they realize that they live right next to each other. The film's plot takes a turn and the students document their surprise.
